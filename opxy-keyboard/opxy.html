OP-XY Keyboard

OP-XY Keyboard

OVERVIEW

This project is entirely independent. I've always admired the design behind Teenage Engineerings music inventions and deciced to take a shot at recreating one of their products and animating it for an Ad.

YEAR

2025

TOOLS

Blender
A. Illustrator
A. Photoshop
A. Premier Pro

SERVICES

3D Modeling & Rendering
Product Animation


About the project

Goal

After modeling the keyboard as closely as possible in Blender, I drew the UI elements on Illustrator to later apply them to the respective parts of the keyboard. For the Advertising animation, I wanted to reflect the experimental essence of the keyboard while keeping its clean and profissional design look.
